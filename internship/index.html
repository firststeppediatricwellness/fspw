    Internship Guide & Support

    01

    Complete Your Report

    Fill in all mandatory fields accurately. Use professional language.
    Each section below requires 3 points each.

    1. Activities Summary: List the main tasks you performed today. (e.g., "Analysed a case study on market research").
    2. Key Concepts: What were the main theories or terms learned? (e.g., "SWOT Analysis", "Neural Coupling").
    3. Activity Notes: Details about specific group work or individual exercises you handled.
    4. Impactful Learning: What was the most important takeaway for you personally?
    5. Challenges: Mention any difficulties (technical or conceptual) and how you navigated them.
    6. Application: How will you use these specific skills in your future career or projects?
